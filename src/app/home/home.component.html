About our Campuses!

Citadel Institute of Medical Sciences (CIMEDS) Port Villa, Vanuatu

The first campus of CIMEDS is located at Port Vila, Vanuatu. The Vanuatu archipelago is an island country situated in the South Pacific Ocean off the coast of Northern Australia, surrounded by New Guinea and Fiji on either side.

Along with the campus in Vanuatu, CIMEDS also has constituent campuses coming up in Bangladesh, Indonesia, and India. Upcoming projects also include having new campuses in UAE, Fiji, and Australia.

Why CIMEDS?

CIMEDS brings to medical education, a unique combination of Online Self Learning, Live Virtual Classes, and Campus Immersion, a learning methodology that helps scale up the number of learners across regions, without compromising the quality of learning. This opens up doors of medical education to learners who miss out due to lack of seats or economic background.

Early Clinical Exposure

We will have technological tools for learners to get an early access to clinics and clinical practices. This will be extended to initiatives like Student-Run.

Online Learning

Use of world class Learning Management System (LMS) to keep learners engaged, share content, track progress and drive peer-to-peer learning.

Live Virtual Classes

Learners not in campus will have regular classes with the faculty through live virtual platform for coverage of critical concepts, mentorship and doubt clearing.

Campus Immersion

Learners will spend initial part of the off-campus time with our global partner hospital for practical learning and the last few years at one of our campus locations.

Explore Our Programs

Our educational programs advance CIMEDS Medical School’s core mission to alleviate human suffering by nurturing a diverse group of leaders and future leaders in both clinical care and biomedical inquiry. These individuals are on the front lines of medicine and science serving individuals and populations locally, nationally, and globally.

Global MD Program

A global MD Program, aligned to USMLE, supplemented with Pre-Medical training module taken remotely or at the campus, while Pre-Clinical and Clinical modules are taken at one of our campuses with special preps for the USMLE Step 1 and Step 2 exams.

know more!

MBBS Program

A campus-based program whose curriculum is aligned to NMC - India, with a primary focus on Indian sub-continents, conducted at the designated Global Campuses followed by an Internship at one of the partner hospitals. Post the program students are eligible for Foreign Medical Graduates (FMG) Exam in India.

know more!

USMLE Program

The program enables MBBS Graduates to earn an MD qualification and target medical residency programs in countries like the US, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, and the Middle East. The program also allows an option to prepare for the Australian Medical Council Exams (AMC).

know more!

Pre Medical

Nursing Qualification Program will cater to the vast requirement of nursing professionals globally. The program will be conducted in blended mode with the support of leading technologies and facilities of our partner hospitals.

About CiMEDs

This combination makes medical education more inclusive towards learners who miss out due to lack of financial support or resources.

CIMEDS aims to make a significant addition to the number of well-trained, world-class medical professionals, who will contribute towards the healthcare and wellbeing of society.

Citadel Institute of Medical Sciences (CIMEDS) Port Villa, Vanuatu

The first campus of CIMEDS is located at Port Vila, Vanuatu. The Vanuatu archipelago is an island country situated in the South Pacific Ocean off the coast of Northern Australia, surrounded by New Guinea and Fiji on either side.

Along with the campus in Vanuatu, CIMEDS also has constituent campuses coming up in Bangladesh, Indonesia, and India. Upcoming projects also include having new campuses in UAE, Fiji, and Australia.

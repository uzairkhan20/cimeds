CIMEDS brings to medical education, a unique blend of Live Virtual Classes, and Campus Immersion, with a learning methodology that helps scale up the number of learners across regions, without compromising the quality of learning. This opens up doors of medical education to learners who miss out due to lack of seats or economic background.

Early Clinical Exposure

A unique tech-based solution for CIMEDS students to shadow telemedicine sessions led by licensed medical practitioners, followed by a round table discussion on the case with the experts. The participation will help early exposure to clinical cases and will help earn relevant credits.

Early Surgery Exposure

A unique tech platform to enable learners to look inside a live Surgery Room remotely, interact with the surgeon during the procedure, and refer to the recorded session with narration in the future.

Live Virtual Classes

For the off-campus modules, learners will have regular classes with the faculty through a live virtual platform for coverage of non-clinical concepts, mentorship, and doubt clearing. Learners will also access a world-class LMS to share content, track progress, engage with others and benefit from peer-to-peer learning.

Campus Immersion

Learners will spend initial part of the off-campus time with our global partner hospital for practical learning and the last few years at one of our campus locations.

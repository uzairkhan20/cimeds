Global MD Program

Global MD

The MD Program is a global program that provides modern western-style medical education; preparation for USMLE® is one of the priorities of the program. The structure of the program facilitates and equips learners with the skills and knowledge to pass USMLE® Step 1, Step 2 before residency.

The program prepares learners to become competitive Medical Doctors with global exposure, ready to enter any postgraduate or residency medical training program. The learners get a minimum of 3 hours per week of clinical observations/ rotations right from the beginning of the program, to get exposed to the clinical settings and patient care from a very early stage.

The MD program delivered by CIMEDS is aligned to USMLE and prepares the learners for achieving residency and licensure in the United States, Canada, New Zealand, Australia, and the Middle East. The curriculum is designed with the same pre-requisites as US students require, to offer students a strong foundation required for Step 1, Step 2 exams, and Residency. It is an integrated, systems-based curriculum that parallels the training received at a top US Medical School. Prospective students can join the program at different stages according to the credits accumulated via their previous degree(s) or upon completing high school and therefore all students will have a personalized duration of study that matches the US system of medical education.

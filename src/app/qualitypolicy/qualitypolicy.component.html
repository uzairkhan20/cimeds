Quality Policy

The Quality Policy of CIMEDS is as follows:

  1. Commits to educate the new generation of medical professionals in a collaborative learning environment with the highest quality of medical education by granting them the best grounds of basic medical knowledge.
  2. To promote and cultivate learning by research through technological support and expertise to transformative knowledge to promote the quality of health and life for humanity.
  3. Pledges to support the development of its students into highly successful and respected physicians.
  4. Instils, the quality of CIMEDS in its students who will be instrumental in guiding peers of the medical community across the globe and contribute towards building healthier societies.
  5. To provide accessible , innovative health and wellness care services to improve health outcomes through science, technology and advanced research.

Citadel Institute of Medical Sciences (CIMEDS)

About CiMEDs

CIMEDS brings to medical education, a unique combination of Online Self Learning, Live Virtual Classes, and Campus Immersion, a learning methodology that helps scale up the number of learners across regions, without compromising the quality of learning. This combination makes medical education more inclusive towards learners who miss out due to lack of financial support or resources.

CIMEDS aims to make a significant addition to the number of well-trained, world-class medical professionals, who will contribute towards the healthcare and wellbeing of society.

Campuses and Hospitals

Citadel Institute of Medical Sciences (CIMEDS) first campus is located at Port Villa - Vanuatu with branch campus set up in Bangladesh, India and a joint MBBS program at a partner campus in Fiji.

Future campuses will be established in UAE, Indonesia, and Australia over the next 2 years. All CIMEDS campuses will carry ECFMG and local Government approvals.

About the Group

MedComm Education and Healthcare Consultancy LLC has been established by a group of passionate professionals from the fields of Healthcare, Education and Technology from across the globe. Experts from US, Gulf, India and Australia have come together and set up the head office in Dubai as a global base. MedComm intends to set up Hospitals, Teaching Campuses and Research Centers across countries in South Pacific, Gulf, Africa, Asia and Central Europe.

MedComm’s primary focus is to make medical education scalable, economical, inclusive and add significant number of trained medical professionals to serve the society. MedComm also intends to be a one-stop consulting service provider for various requirements in Healthcare, Research, Education, Pharma and Medical Technology for entities like NGOs, State Governments and Start-ups in the domain.

The first campus of CIMEDS is located at Port Vila, Vanuatu. The Vanuatu archipelago is an island country situated in the South Pacific Ocean off the coast of Northern Australia, surrounded by New Guinea and Fiji on either side.

Along with the campus in Vanuatu, CIMEDS also has constituent campuses coming up in Bangladesh, Indonesia, and India. Upcoming projects also include having new campuses in UAE, Fiji, and Australia.
